  CGB was "traumatising" at RPE 9. Delaying comp bench until shoulder confidence rebuilds.
- **No dips.** Block 1 W1 dip was +70@9 vs plan +25-35@6-7, then dropped W3-W4. Removed.
- **Squat is primary on D1.** Block 1 squat routinely overperformed (445@8.5 vs plan
  445@7.5-8). Leaning into it.
- **Cable Curl replaces Hammer Curl** — elbow-safe.
- **RDL + OHP on D2**, paused squat stays tempo day on D3.
